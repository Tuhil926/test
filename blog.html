IITH has a dedicated guidance and counselling unit and student mentors who can help and offer advice in all kinds of matters. Since its inception on January 12, 2012, Sunshine- the counselling cell at IITH, has been committed to helping the student community. The dedicated team of Sunshine comprises a faculty in-charge, psychological counsellors, faculty representatives, student heads, management team members and student mentors. The Sunshine cell also runs the Student Mentorship Program. This program is aimed at offering the incoming students an interface to understand and interact with the diverse student community at IITH. With the institute having a strict anti-ragging policy, the program also doubles up as an excellent ice-breaker.
